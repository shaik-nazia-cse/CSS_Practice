Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter & cream
  • Masala spices

Steps

  1. Cook tomato puree with spices.
  2. Add paneer & butter.
  3. Add cream & serve.

Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup maida
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • Sugar syrup

Steps

  1. Mix maida, corn flour & curd.
  2. Rest 1 hour.
  3. Fry spirals.
  4. Dip in sugar syrup.
